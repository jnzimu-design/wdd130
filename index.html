Kinshasa

Kinshasa is the capital and largest city of the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Located along the Congo River, it is one of Africa's biggest and most vibrant urban centers. Kinshasa is known for its rich culture, lively music scene—especially styles like rumba—and its role as the political, economic, and cultural heart of the country.
